We're a month and a half into 2026. I just finished <cite>Rhythm of War</cite> – book four of <cite>[The Stormlight Archive](tab:https://www.brandonsanderson.com/pages/the-stormlight-archive-series)</cite> by Brandon Sanderson. It's a book about mental health, disguised as an epic fantasy. I absolutely loved it and I can't recommend Brandon Sanderson's <cite>Cosmere</cite> books highly enough.

In a much sadder update, my grandma passed away last month. Her funeral was earlier this week and my brother and I were pallbearers. I'm a crying man, so there's no way I could have held myself together to say anything as part of the service. Being a pallbearer was my way of being involved in her funeral.

We've booked a couple of holidays for this year. A winter break in Poland and a spring trip in northern Italy. After how busy last year was, it's been nice to slow down over winter and spend a few weekends at home. I'd like both of these holidays to have a comfortable pace – not boring, not stressful.
