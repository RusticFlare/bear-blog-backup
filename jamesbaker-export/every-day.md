Since the start of 2026 I’ve been trying to do a few things every day:
* Read
* Exercise
* Create

## Read

Reading most days is something I do anyway. I’ve read in bed before falling asleep since I was a child. I'm also counting listening to audiobooks.

## Exercise

This is the one I struggle with the most. I still exercise most days, and some of that is built into my routine; I run home from the office twice a week, play 5-a-side on a Tuesday. 

I find it most difficult to exercise when I’m out of my routine. Especially when I take into account the time it takes to get ready before, and shower afterwards.

I also count going for walks, but January in the UK isn't the best time of year for that.

## Create  

At the moment, I'm trying to post an [image on this blog](/images/) every day. So far all of those images have been from creative coding using [OPENRNDR](tab:https://openrndr.org/), but yesterday I got a [Camp Snap Pro](tab:https://www.campsnapphoto.com/products/cs-pro-camera), so maybe some photos will start showing up as well.

## Closing thoughts

My goals and how I want to spend my time may change, but the plan right now is to build these habits.
